Did you know only 4 species of big cats roar? Lions, tigers, leopards and jaguars can roar thanks to a specialized larynx and hyoid apparatus, with lions being the loudest. Lion roars can be heard 8 to 10 km away! Cats that can roar, can't purr while cats that can't roar, can purr.
